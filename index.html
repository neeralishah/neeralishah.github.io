    About Me

    "Fail fast. Fail hard. Fail Often." - Reshma Saujani CEO of Girls Who Code
    My purpose is to learn and help others along the way. I'm an active student in our Computer Science department at Purdue University. Serving as an executive member of the Undergraduate Computer Science Board and teaching assistant for a freshman seminar, I hope to build programs and instruction in courses to improve retention rates of underrepresented groups in Computer Science.
    My DISC ratings are S/I, which stand for Support and Inspiration. I hope to mitigate the fear often associated with working in the tech world. Making the environment inclusive and encouraging from the university level will allow for a diverse and equitable industry environment.
    Apart from my efforts to close the gender gap in tech, I hope to lead a career in Fintech. Math and economics have always been intriguing and favorite subjects of mine; with my knowledge in CS I hope to work on the crossroads of business and technology.
